FAQ - ATS E-SHOPPING WEBSITE

To place an order, simply browse our products, add the items you wish to purchase to your shopping cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your shipping and payment information, and your order will be processed.
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. You can select your preferred payment method during checkout.
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and delivery of your package through our website or the shipping carrier's website.
Once your order has been placed, it is immediately sent to our processing team to ensure quick fulfillment. Therefore, we are unable to cancel or change orders after they have been submitted. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Please review our return policy for more details.
